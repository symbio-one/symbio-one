ABOUT THE COMPANY

Anhui Huangshan Capsule Co.,Ltd. was founded in 1989, it is a large-scale capasule manufacturer engaged in producing hollow capsules and enteric hollow capsules.
The company is located in the world famous scenic spot Mount Huangshan, covering an area of 246,000 square meters, with a production plant area of 41,600 square meters. The purification level of the clean area meets the D-class standard of GMP requirements. There are more than 700 employees. It has 72 production lines of various hollow capsules.
